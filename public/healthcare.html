Our Services

We provide a wide range of medical services to support the health and wellness of our students, faculty, and staff

Primary Care

Comprehensive medical consultations for illness, injuries, and routine health assessments by experienced physicians.

Emergency Care

Immediate treatment for injuries, accidents, and acute medical conditions with fully equipped first aid facilities.

Mental Health

Confidential counseling services, stress management programs, and mental health awareness workshops.

Immunizations

Vaccinations including flu shots, travel vaccines, and routine immunizations to protect community health.

Wellness Programs

Health education seminars, nutrition counseling, and preventive health screenings for optimal wellbeing.

Referral Services

Coordination with specialist providers and hospitals for advanced diagnostic and treatment needs.
