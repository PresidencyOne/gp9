Know more about PresidencyOne

Discover Your Campus Like Never Before!

PresidencyOne is a dedicated platform designed to provide students,faculty and visitors with comprehensive information about university events, campus facilities, and key locations. From academic conferences and cultural festivals to student-led initiatives and workshops, stay informed about every important event happening across the university. In addition to event updates, Presidency One offers detailed insights into essential campus facilities, food stalls, stationery and more.

Whether you need to locate a professor's office, find the best dining options on campus, or check the schedule for an upcoming event, our platform ensures you have all the information at your fingertips. Enhance your university experience with Presidency One—your trusted source for staying connected, informed, and engaged with campus life.

Stationery

A well-equipped stationery shop is fundamental for effective studying and organization. Students should maintain a collection of basic supplies including notebooks, pens, pencils, and highlighters for note-taking, along with sticky notes for efficient revision. Organization becomes seamless with binders, folders, and file organizers to keep important documents accessible. For STEM courses, a scientific calculator proves indispensable, while planners or digital scheduling tools help manage academic deadlines and commitments.
