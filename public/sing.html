About Us

Welcome to the Presidency University Singing Club, where passion meets melody! Established in 2015, our club is a vibrant community of music enthusiasts dedicated to celebrating the art of singing. From classical to contemporary, we embrace all genres and encourage every voice to shine.

Our mission is to foster creativity, build confidence, and create unforgettable memories through music. Whether you're a seasoned vocalist or a beginner, our club offers a supportive environment to grow and connect with fellow singers.
