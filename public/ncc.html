Presidency University NCC Club

Welcome to the National Cadet Corps (NCC) Club of Presidency University. Our club is dedicated to fostering leadership, discipline, and patriotism among students. We engage in various activities such as drills, camps, community service, and adventure training to develop well-rounded individuals. The NCC (National Cadet Corps) Club of Presidency University is a vibrant and disciplined student organization that aims to develop character, leadership, and a sense of patriotic service among the youth. Through regular training sessions, parades, camps, and social service activities, the club fosters a spirit of unity, responsibility, and selfless contribution. Cadets actively participate in national-level events and represent the university with pride and honor. The NCC Club plays a crucial role in shaping well-rounded individuals who are physically fit, mentally strong, and dedicated to serving the nation.
