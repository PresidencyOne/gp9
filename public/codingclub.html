About Us

The Presidency University Coding Club is a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts dedicated to fostering coding skills, innovation, and collaboration. Established in 2018, we aim to empower students with the knowledge and skills to excel in the tech world.

Our mission is to create a platform where students can learn, code, and build projects that make a difference. From hackathons to workshops, we provide opportunities to explore various domains like AI, Web Development, and Competitive Programming.
