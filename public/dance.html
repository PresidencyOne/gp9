About Our Groovy Gang

Founded in 2018, the Presidency University Dance Club is where passion meets performance! We’re a colorful crew that celebrates every dance form—Bollywood, hip-hop, contemporary, classical, and beyond. Our mission? To spread joy, one dance move at a time! Whether you’re a pro or just love to wiggle, we’re here to make every step count.

The Dance Club at Presidency University, Bengaluru, is a dynamic and inclusive platform that nurtures students' passion for dance across various styles, from classical to contemporary. The club actively participates in major university events, such as the annual cultural fest INVINCIA and Euphoria, showcasing their talent and contributing to the vibrant campus culture. Notably, the club collaborates with other student organizations, like the Fashion Club, to create engaging performances that celebrate cultural diversity and artistic expression. Through regular workshops, performances, and competitions, the Dance Club fosters a sense of community, creativity, and enthusiasm among its members, enriching the student experience at Presidency University.

  • Workshops Galore: Learn epic moves every week!
  • Showstopper Events: Our annual "Dance Dhamaka" is a blast!
  • Battle It Out: Compete in inter-college dance-offs!
  • Besties for Life: Join a squad that vibes together!
