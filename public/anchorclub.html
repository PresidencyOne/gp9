Anchors & Speakers Club

The Presidency University Anchors & Speakers Club (PUASC) is a vibrant community dedicated to fostering public speaking, anchoring, and leadership skills among students. Established in 2018, our club provides a platform for students to express themselves, build confidence, and master the art of communication.

Our mission is to empower individuals to articulate their thoughts effectively and inspire others through impactful speeches, debates, and hosting events. We organize workshops, competitions, and interactive sessions to hone skills and create memorable experiences.
