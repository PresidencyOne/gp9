Complaint Box

Welcome to the Presidency University Complaint Box — a digital platform created to ensure that every student, faculty, and staff member has a voice.

This initiative is part of a student-led capstone project aimed at enhancing communication and transparency within the university. Our goal is to provide a safe, user-friendly space where concerns, suggestions, and feedback can be submitted efficiently and responsibly.

Whether it’s an issue related to academics, campus facilities, student services, or general well-being — this platform is here to make sure your voice is heard by the right people. Together, we can build a better, more responsive Presidency University.
