About Us

Established in 2018, the Presidency University Gaming Club is a thriving community for gamers of all kinds. Whether you're into esports, casual gaming, or game development, we have a place for you. Our club organizes tournaments, workshops, and social events to bring students together. Join us to compete, create, and connect in a fun, inclusive environment!
