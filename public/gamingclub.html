About Us

Established in 2018, the Presidency University Gaming Club is a thriving community for gamers of all kinds. Whether you're into esports, casual gaming, or game development, we have a place for you. Our club organizes tournaments, workshops, and social events to bring students together. Join us to compete, create, and connect in a fun, inclusive environment! The club also provides a platform for budding game designers and tech enthusiasts to collaborate and innovate. With regular hackathons, game jams, and mentoring sessions, members get exposure to the gaming industry and its evolving trends. Our mission is to foster creativity, sportsmanship, and technical skills through the love of gaming.
