Welcome to Blossom

Your space for mental wellness and growth at Presidency University.

Nurturing Well-being Together

At Blossom, we believe that mental wellness is fundamental to academic success and personal fulfillment. We provide a supportive, confidential, and inclusive environment where students of Presidency University can find resources, guidance, and community to navigate life's challenges and flourish. Whether you're seeking counseling, workshops, or just a quiet space to reflect, Blossom is here for you.

About Blossom

Our Mission

Blossom is dedicated to fostering a campus culture at Presidency University that prioritizes and promotes mental health and emotional well-being. We strive to empower students with the skills and support needed to navigate academic pressures, personal growth, and life transitions successfully.

Our Values

  • Confidentiality: Your privacy is paramount in all our interactions.
  • Inclusivity: We welcome and support students from all backgrounds.
  • Compassion: We approach every individual with empathy and understanding.
  • Empowerment: We aim to equip students with tools for resilience and self-care.

Our Services

Individual Counseling

Confidential one-on-one sessions with trained counselors to discuss personal concerns, stress, anxiety, or any other challenges.

Workshops & Training

Interactive sessions on topics like stress management, mindfulness, communication skills, and building healthy relationships.

Support Groups

Peer support groups provide a safe space to share experiences and connect with others facing similar challenges.

Crisis Intervention

Immediate support and resources for students experiencing a mental health crisis.

Wellness Check-ins

Brief, informal check-ins to discuss well-being and connect you with appropriate resources if needed.

Referrals

Connecting students to specialized off-campus mental health professionals or resources when necessary.
