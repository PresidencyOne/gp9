About NCC Club

The NCC (National Cadet Corps) is a voluntary youth organization in India, open to school and college students. It's a Tri-Services organization, comprising the Army, Navy, and Air Force, aimed at developing the youth into disciplined and patriotic citizens. NCC cadets receive basic military training, including small arms and drills.
