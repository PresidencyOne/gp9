About Us

The Toastmasters Club of Presidency University is a vibrant community dedicated to enhancing public speaking, leadership, and communication skills. Founded in [Year], our club provides a supportive environment where students and professionals alike can grow, network, and excel.

Our meetings are filled with engaging speeches, impromptu speaking sessions, and constructive feedback, all designed to help members become confident communicators and effective leaders.

​The Toastmasters Club at Presidency University, Bengaluru, officially chartered on August 14, 2023, serves as a dynamic platform for students to enhance their public speaking and leadership skills. Meeting every Friday at 2:30 PM on campus, the club offers a supportive environment where members can practice effective communication, receive constructive feedback, and build confidence. As part of Toastmasters International, a global non-profit organization dedicated to communication and leadership development, the club aligns with the mission to provide a mutually supportive and positive learning environment. Since its inauguration on September 8, 2023, the club has actively engaged students in various speaking and leadership activities, contributing to their personal and professional growth. For more information or to get involved, interested individuals can contact the club at 9731857937 or visit the Presidency University campus in Itgalpura, Rajanukunte, Yelahanka, Bengaluru
